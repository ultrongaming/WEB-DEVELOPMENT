The world of cricket and featured players


MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian professional cricketer who plays as a right-handed batter and a wicket-keeper.

Kumar Sangakkara

Kumar Chokshanada Sangakkara is a Sri Lankan former professional cricketer who represented Sri Lanka from 2000 to 2015. A former captain in all formats.

James Anderson

James Michael Anderson is an English cricketer who played for England. He is currently serving as the fast bowling mentor of the England cricket team.

Brian Lara

Brian Lara is a Trinidadian former international cricketer, widely acknowledged as one of the greatest batsmen of all time.

Gary Kirsten

Gary Kirsten is a South African cricket coach and former cricketer. He is currently the head coach of Pakistan national cricket team.

Daniel Vettori

Daniel Luca Vettori ONZM is a New Zealand cricket coach and former cricketer who played for the New Zealand national cricket team

Gary Wilson

Gary Craig Wilson is a former Irish cricketer, who is a wicket-keeper and right-handed batsman. He played for Ireland in Tests, One Day and Twenty20.

Babar Azam

Mohammad Babar Azam is a Pakistani international cricketer and the current captain of the Pakistan national cricket team in limited overs formats.

Andrew Flower

Andrew Flower is a Zimbabwean cricketer ever and one of the greatest wicket-keeper-batters of all time.

Rashid Khan

Rashid Khan Arman is an Afghan international cricketer and captain of the Afghanistan national team in the T20I format.

Mushfiqur Rahim

Mushfiqur Rahim is a Bangladeshi cricketer and is a right-handed middle-order batter and wicket-keeper in the national team.

George Dockrell

George Henry Dockrell is an Irish cricketer, who plays international cricket for Ireland, having learned his cricket at Leinster Cricket Club, Dublin
